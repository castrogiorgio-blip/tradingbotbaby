RISK CONSIDERATIONS & MITIGATIONS
================================================================================

RISK 1: Execution Risk (Tight Stops)
  Description: Tight stops require quick order execution
  Severity: Medium
  Mitigation:
    - Use limit orders instead of market orders
    - Provide adequate time buffers (5-10 min)
    - Test with small position sizes first
    - Use automated alerts at 1.2x credit level

RISK 2: Whipsaw Risk (Dynamic Sizing)
  Description: May reduce exposure right before recovery
  Severity: Low-Medium
  Mitigation:
    - Use weekly rebalancing instead of daily
    - Add hysteresis bands (e.g., VIX 18-22 instead of 20)
    - Document all rebalancing events
    - Review quarterly for false signals

RISK 3: Put Hedging Basis Risk
  Description: Put delta/gamma may not perfectly offset spreads
  Severity: Low
  Mitigation:
    - Use 50% position coverage (not 100%)
    - Accept partial protection
    - Test put effectiveness in paper trading
    - Adjust OTM percentage if needed

RISK 4: Market Regime Change
  Description: Volatility assumptions may fundamentally change
  Severity: Medium
  Mitigation:
    - Conduct quarterly strategy review
    - Monitor volatility regime indicators
    - Adjust VIX thresholds if needed
    - Document any major market structure changes

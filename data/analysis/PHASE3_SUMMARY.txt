================================================================================
RISK CONSIDERATIONS
================================================================================

1. Execution Risk
   - Tight stops require quick order execution to avoid slippage
   - Mitigation: Use limit orders, adequate time buffers

2. Whipsaw Risk
   - Dynamic sizing may reduce exposure right before recovery
   - Mitigation: Use weekly rebalancing instead of daily

3. Put Hedging Basis Risk
   - Put delta/gamma may not perfectly offset credit spread losses
   - Mitigation: Use 50% of position size, accept partial protection

4. Regime Change
   - If volatility regime fundamentally changes, all hedges may fail
   - Mitigation: Quarterly review of strategy assumptions
